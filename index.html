Research Intern

Undergraduate Research Assistant

I worked with Professor Paulo Carvalho under CMU LearnLab to investigate how how using different active learning techniques impacts student learning. I cleaned and transformed student performance data from seven semesters, created data visualizations, and conduct a meta-analysis to identify differences between POE (Predict-Observe-Explain) and a baseline active learning method.

Data Science Intern

I supported PA 211's statewide initiative in assisting thousands of people in need of shelter, food, and utilities by developing BI dashboards and data reports in DOMO.
